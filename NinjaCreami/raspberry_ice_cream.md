# Raspberry Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen raspberries | 150 g |
| Whole milk | 150 g |
| Heavy cream | 150 g |
| Sugar | 55 g |
| Corn syrup | 15 g |
| Salt | Pinch |
| Xanthan gum | 1/16 tsp |

### Method

1. Heat the milk, sugar, corn syrup, and salt until the sugar dissolves.
2. Blend in the frozen raspberries until completely smooth.
3. Strain through a fine mesh sieve to remove seeds.
4. Stir in the heavy cream.
5. Sprinkle in the xanthan gum while blending for 15–20 seconds.
6. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.
