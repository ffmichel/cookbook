# Chocolate Brownie Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Unsweetened cocoa powder | 50 g |
| Whole milk, warmed | 73 g |
| Sweetened condensed milk | 189 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 146 g |
| Vanilla extract | 5 g |
| Salt | Pinch |
| Xanthan gum | 0.5 g |

### Method

1. Whisk warm milk and cocoa powder into a smooth paste in the pint container.
2. Add condensed milk, heavy cream, vanilla, salt, and xanthan gum. Whisk until combined.
3. Freeze 15–24 hours without the lid (keeps the top smooth).

### Spin Settings

Run **Ice Cream**. If crumbly, add a splash of cream to the center and **Respin**.

### Brownie Mix-In

- Use fudgy brownies, not cakey — drier brownies freeze poorly around the chunks.
- Cut into ½-inch chunks, about ¾ cup.
- Make a well in the center, add brownie chunks, run **Mix-In** cycle.
- Add a pinch of flaky salt on top before serving.
- If too soft after respinning, freeze 10–15 minutes before serving.
