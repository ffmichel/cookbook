# Peach Mango Sorbet (dairy-free)

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen peach chunks | 284 g |
| Frozen mango chunks | 116 g |
| Sugar | 100 g |
| Lemon juice | 20 g |
| Water | 30 g |
| Xanthan gum | 2 g |

### Method

1. Combine peaches, mango, sugar, lemon juice, water, and xanthan gum in a blender.
2. Blend until completely smooth.
3. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze at least 24 hours.

### Spin Settings

Run **Sorbet**.
