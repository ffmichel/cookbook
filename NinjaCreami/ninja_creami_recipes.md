# Ninja Creami Recipes

*Store-bought style ice cream results — no protein powder, no pudding mix.*

---

## Strawberry Swirl

**Yield:** 1 pint

**Use:** Strawberry swirl mix-in.

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen strawberries | 400 g |
| Sugar | 100 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 70 g |
| Cream cheese | 70 g |
| Xanthan gum | 1 g |

### Method

1. Blend the frozen strawberries, sugar, heavy cream, cream cheese, and xanthan gum together until completely smooth (no heating needed).
2. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.

## Peach Mango Sorbet (dairy-free)

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen peach chunks | 284 g |
| Frozen mango chunks | 116 g |
| Sugar | 100 g |
| Lemon juice | 20 g |
| Water | 30 g |
| Xanthan gum | 2 g |

### Method

1. Combine peaches, mango, sugar, lemon juice, water, and xanthan gum in a blender.
2. Blend until completely smooth.
3. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze at least 24 hours.

### Spin Settings

Run **Sorbet**.

## Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Whole milk | 200 g |
| Heavy cream | 120 g |
| Sweetened condensed milk | 85 g |
| Vanilla extract | 1/2 tsp |
| Oreo cookies | 4–5 |
| Xanthan gum | 1 g |

### Method

1. Mix milk, cream, condensed milk, and vanilla.
2. Mix in the xanthan gum.
3. Pour into the Creami pint container.
4. Freeze 24 hours with the lid off.
5. Spin on Ice Cream.
6. If powdery, add 1 tbsp milk and Re-Spin.
7. Add broken Oreos and run Mix-In.

## Strawberry Sorbet

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen strawberries | 400 g |
| Sugar | 150 g |
| Lemon juice | 25 g |
| Xanthan gum | 2 g |

### Method

1. Combine strawberries, sugar, lemon juice, and xanthan gum in the Thermomix.
2. Blend for 4 minutes at 50°C until smooth.
3. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.

### Spin Settings

Run **Sorbet**.

### Notes

- No salt in this recipe.

## Raspberry Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen raspberries | 150 g |
| Whole milk | 150 g |
| Heavy cream | 150 g |
| Sugar | 55 g |
| Corn syrup | 15 g |
| Salt | Pinch |
| Xanthan gum | 1/16 tsp |

### Method

1. Heat the milk, sugar, corn syrup, and salt until the sugar dissolves.
2. Blend in the frozen raspberries until completely smooth.
3. Strain through a fine mesh sieve to remove seeds.
4. Stir in the heavy cream.
5. Sprinkle in the xanthan gum while blending for 15–20 seconds.
6. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.

## Chocolate Brownie Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Unsweetened cocoa powder | 50 g |
| Whole milk, warmed | 73 g |
| Sweetened condensed milk | 189 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 146 g |
| Vanilla extract | 5 g |
| Salt | Pinch |
| Xanthan gum | 0.5 g |

### Method

1. Whisk warm milk and cocoa powder into a smooth paste in the pint container.
2. Add condensed milk, heavy cream, vanilla, salt, and xanthan gum. Whisk until combined.
3. Freeze 15–24 hours without the lid (keeps the top smooth).

### Spin Settings

Run **Ice Cream**. If crumbly, add a splash of cream to the center and **Respin**.

### Brownie Mix-In

- Use fudgy brownies, not cakey — drier brownies freeze poorly around the chunks.
- Cut into ½-inch chunks, about ¾ cup.
- Make a well in the center, add brownie chunks, run **Mix-In** cycle.
- Add a pinch of flaky salt on top before serving.
- If too soft after respinning, freeze 10–15 minutes before serving.
