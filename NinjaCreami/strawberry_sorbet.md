# Strawberry Sorbet

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen strawberries | 400 g |
| Sugar | 150 g |
| Lemon juice | 25 g |
| Xanthan gum | 2 g |

### Method

1. Combine strawberries, sugar, lemon juice, and xanthan gum in the Thermomix.
2. Blend for 4 minutes at 50°C until smooth.
3. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.

### Spin Settings

Run **Sorbet**.

### Notes

- No salt in this recipe.
