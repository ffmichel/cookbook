# Ninja Creami Preferences

When suggesting or discussing Ninja Creami recipes, follow these preferences:

- **No high-protein recipes** — avoid anything designed around protein powder or boosting protein content
- **No pudding mix** — do not use instant pudding mix as an ingredient
- **Goal is store-bought style** — recipes should aim to replicate the texture and taste of commercial ice cream, not health food
- **Xanthan gum is fine** — acceptable as a stabilizer
