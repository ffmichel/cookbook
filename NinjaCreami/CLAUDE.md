# Ninja Creami Project

This folder contains Ninja Creami ice cream recipes and preferences.

## Preferences

- No high-protein recipes — avoid protein powder or anything designed to boost protein
- No pudding mix — do not use instant pudding mix
- Goal is store-bought style — replicate the texture and taste of commercial ice cream, not health food
- Xanthan gum is fine as a stabilizer

## Files

- `ninja_creami_recipes.md` — all recipes
- `ninja_creami_preferences.md` — full preferences reference
