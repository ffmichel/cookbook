# Strawberry Swirl

**Yield:** 1 pint

**Use:** Strawberry swirl mix-in.

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen strawberries | 400 g |
| Sugar | 100 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 70 g |
| Cream cheese | 70 g |
| Xanthan gum | 1 g |

### Method

1. Blend the frozen strawberries, sugar, heavy cream, cream cheese, and xanthan gum together until completely smooth (no heating needed).
2. Pour into a Ninja Creami pint and freeze for at least 24 hours.
