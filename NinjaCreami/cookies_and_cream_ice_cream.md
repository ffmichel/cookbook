# Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

**Yield:** 1 pint

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Whole milk | 200 g |
| Heavy cream | 120 g |
| Sweetened condensed milk | 85 g |
| Vanilla extract | 1/2 tsp |
| Oreo cookies | 4–5 |
| Xanthan gum | 1 g |

### Method

1. Mix milk, cream, condensed milk, and vanilla.
2. Mix in the xanthan gum.
3. Pour into the Creami pint container.
4. Freeze 24 hours with the lid off.
5. Spin on Ice Cream.
6. If powdery, add 1 tbsp milk and Re-Spin.
7. Add broken Oreos and run Mix-In.
